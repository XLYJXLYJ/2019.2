The Market
Insurance is a highly manpower intensive industry and is traded through multiple layers of intermediaries. Such inefficiencies are damaging the profitability of most insurance institutions who are now turning to innovative technologies for solutions.
The Innovations
Artificial intelligence is gradually but centainly becoming the trend for our changing insurance industry. Insurance robots, as one of the newest parties involved, is influencing and creating a new insurance eco-system for all players in the chain.
The Solution
Our innovative platform combines AI and insurance together based on blockchain's decentralization and tranparency characheristics, and brings efficienies to a new higeght with references to the Lloyd's blueprint.
